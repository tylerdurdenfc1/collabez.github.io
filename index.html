Who are we?
Collabez is a dedicated platform where you can share your business ideas so that other like-minded individuals can contact and collaborate with you.

Our vision is to help business ideas turn into businesses whilst also helping existing businesses grow through the power of collaboration.
How does it work?
Individuals can submit business ideas using the 'ideas' section. These ideas could also be existing businesses that want to expand.

There is an AUD$2.99 fee for each submission. Once paid, ideas are posted on the site within 12 hours!
